Our Story

From Cart To Coffee Landmark

Coffee Central began as a weekend espresso cart and evolved into a flagship coffee destination in Thiruvalla. We source directly, roast in small batches, and craft menus around seasonal ingredients.

Each new crop release becomes a tasting event with pairing notes and guided brew recommendations.
